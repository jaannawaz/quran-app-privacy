Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [10/03/2025]

Introduction

Welcome to the Quran App ("we," "our," or "the App"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

The Quran App does not collect personal data. However, we store user preferences such as theme settings, bookmarks, and reading positions locally on your device.

2. Automatically Collected Information

- Device Information: We may collect anonymous technical data such as operating system version and system performance for debugging purposes.

- Location Data: The Qibla Finder feature requires location access, which is processed locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

- To provide and enhance App functionality.

- To enable features like Qibla Finder and personalized Quran reading.

- To store user preferences for a better experience.

Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored locally on your device.

- The App does not require an account or collect personal identifiers.

- We implement necessary security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

The App may include links to third-party services (e.g., Makkah Live streaming). These services have their own privacy policies, which we do not control.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

Your Choices

- You can manage permissions (e.g., location access) through device settings.

- You can delete locally stored data anytime through your device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
